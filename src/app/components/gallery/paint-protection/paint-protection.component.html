Paint Protection Film

Max pro

Max Pro is an advanced paint protection film designed to provide maximum security for your vehicle's exterior. This film utilizes cutting-edge polyurethane technology and features a self-healing property, with a self-healing rate of 90%. This means that the film can automatically repair superficial scratches when exposed to higher temperatures. With a thickness of 340 microns, "Max Pro" offers protection that lasts for over 10 years, maintaining your car's new appearance while providing effective protection against contaminants and UV rays for a worry-free driving experience.

Elegance Guard

Elegance Guard is a high-performance protection film that ensures exceptional protection for your vehicle's paintwork. It utilizes advanced technology that allows it to achieve a self-healing rate of up to 85%, meaning it can automatically repair minor scratches. With a thickness of 280 microns, the film provides protection that lasts for over 7 years, helping to maintain your car's shiny appearance while protecting it from contaminants and UV rays.

Professional Shield

Professional Shield is a professionally designed protection film that safeguards your vehicle's paintwork from damage. It features a self-healing property with a rate of up to 75%, allowing it to automatically repair minor scratches. With a thickness of 240 microns, "Professional Shield" provides protection that lasts for over 5 years, ensuring your car maintains an attractive appearance while protecting it from contaminants and UV rays.

Max m matte

Max M Matte is an innovative protection film that provides a matte finish and exceptional protection for your vehicle's paintwork. With a thickness of 290 microns, it is resistant to scratches and UV rays, ensuring a long-lasting beautiful appearance. It comes with a 10-year warranty and is easy to clean, making it an ideal choice for car enthusiasts.
